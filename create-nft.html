Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about creating NFTs

What file formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, SVG for images; MP4, MOV for videos; MP3, WAV for audio; and GLB, GLTF for 3D models.

How much does it cost to mint an NFT?

Gas fees vary by blockchain. With lazy minting, you pay zero upfront costs. Fees are only deducted when your NFT sells.

Can I sell my NFT on other marketplaces?

Yes! Your NFTs are stored on the blockchain and can be traded on any compatible marketplace.

How do royalties work?

Set a percentage (typically 5-10%) that you'll earn on all future sales of your NFT. Automatically paid to your wallet.
