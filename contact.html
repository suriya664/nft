Common Questions

How do I verify my artist profile?

You need to possess a minimum portfolio of 10 high-quality pieces and undergo our identity verification process. Once submitted, our curators will review your application within 48 hours.

What are the platform gas fees?

We utilize Layer 2 scaling solutions to keep gas fees at a minimum. Standard minting fees are approximately 0.002 ETH.

Is my crypto wallet secure?

We never store your private keys. All transactions are handled through your browser wallet (MetaMask/WalletConnect) for peak security.
